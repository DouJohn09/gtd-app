Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 13 June 2026

We want Cleartable to be an easy yes and an easy no. This policy explains how to cancel, when you can get a refund, and your statutory rights as a consumer in the EU/EEA. Payments and refunds are handled by Paddle, our Merchant of Record.

1. Cancelling your subscription

  • You can cancel at any time from Settings → Billing in the app, or via the receipt/management link Paddle emails you.
  • When you cancel, your plan stays active until the end of the period you've already paid for — you keep Pro features until then, and you simply aren't charged again.
  • Cancelling does not delete your data. You can keep using Cleartable on the Free plan, or export and delete your account whenever you like.

2. Your EU/EEA right of withdrawal

As a consumer in the EU/EEA you normally have a 14-day right of withdrawal on online purchases. For digital services that begin immediately, you may be asked at checkout to agree that the service starts right away — which can end the statutory withdrawal right once the service has been fully provided. Regardless of the technicalities, our voluntary policy below is designed to be at least as generous.

3. Our voluntary refund policy

  • New paid subscriptions: if Cleartable isn't right for you, contact us within 14 days of your first payment for a full refund.
  • Founder pre-sale: the discounted annual founder offer (limited to the first 30 buyers) is fully refundable within 30 days of purchase, no questions asked. It's meant as a low-risk way to back the product early.
  • Renewals: annual renewals are not automatically refunded after the period begins, but if you were charged unexpectedly, contact us — we handle these case by case and aim to be fair.
  • Monthly plans: we don't pro-rate partial months, but you keep access until the period ends after cancelling.

4. How refunds are processed

Refunds are issued by Paddle (our Merchant of Record) back to your original payment method, typically within a few business days of approval. Any tax charged is reversed together with the refund.

5. How to request a refund

Email support@cleartable.app from your account email, or use the management link on your Paddle receipt. Tell us the email on your account and we'll take care of it.

Short version: cancel anytime and keep access until your period ends. New subscriptions are refundable for 14 days, and the founder pre-sale is refundable within its stated window. We'd rather give a clean refund than keep an unhappy customer.

6. Contact

Questions? Email support@cleartable.app.
