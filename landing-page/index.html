questions

What people ask first.

When is Cleartable launching? +
Soft launch to the waitlist within weeks, broader release shortly after. Join the waitlist to be notified first — the first 30 buyers can lock in the refundable $30/yr founder price before Pro settles at $36/yr.
Does it work on mobile? +
Yes — Cleartable is a web app that works on any device with a browser. It's installable as an app (PWA) today — pin it to your phone's home screen or desktop, no app store wait.
Do I need to learn a system to use it? +
No. Cleartable is calm and intuitive out of the box — capture a thought and it gets sorted for you. Underneath, it's built on the proven Getting Things Done® workflow, so productivity fans get a system tuned to their method, and everyone else just gets an app that quietly keeps work and life in order.
Is my data private? +
Yes. Your tasks are stored privately, never sold, and never used to train external AI models. AI features call our providers (Groq and OpenAI) on demand only — per their API policies, your input isn't used for training. You can export everything anytime. See our Privacy Policy.
Can I cancel or get a refund? +
Anytime. Cancel from Settings and you keep Pro until your period ends — no charge after that. New subscriptions are refundable for 14 days, and the founder pre-sale is fully refundable within its launch window. Details in our Refund Policy.
Can I export my data? +
Always. Full JSON backup and Todoist-compatible CSV export are built in. Your data is yours — leave anytime, no lock-in.
Why not just use Todoist or Things? +
Most task apps just store what you type — you still do all the sorting and all the deciding. Cleartable uses AI to organize your inbox, plan your day into realistic time blocks around your calendar, and prepare your weekly review — in one calm place instead of three apps. It's cross-platform (Things and OmniFocus are Apple-only), and Pro comes in under Todoist Pro.
